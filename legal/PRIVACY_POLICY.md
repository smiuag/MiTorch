# TorchZhyla Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** April 24, 2026

## Overview

TorchZhyla is a Telnet/MUD client application. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information.

## Information We Collect

**We do NOT collect or transmit any personal data.**

All user information is stored **locally on your device only**:
- Server configurations (host, port, credentials)
- Button layouts and macros
- Channel aliases
- Application settings (theme, font size, accessibility preferences)
- Terminal history (lines displayed)

## What We Do NOT Do

- ❌ We do not collect your name, email, phone number, or any personal identifying information
- ❌ We do not track your location, contacts, or calendar
- ❌ We do not upload your data to our servers
- ❌ We do not sync your data with cloud services
- ❌ We do not share your data with third parties
- ❌ We do not store analytics or usage data
- ❌ We do not access other apps on your device

## Permissions We Request

TorchZhyla requests the following Android permissions:

| Permission | Purpose |
|-----------|---------|
| `INTERNET` | Required to connect to Telnet/MUD servers |
| `FOREGROUND_SERVICE` | Required to keep the connection alive when app is in background |
| `FOREGROUND_SERVICE_DATA_SYNC` | Required for maintaining data sync while in background |
| `POST_NOTIFICATIONS` | To notify you of connection status and configurable in-game alerts |
| `WAKE_LOCK` | To prevent device from sleeping while connected to a server |
| `VIBRATE` | For optional notification vibration on configurable in-game alerts |
| `MODIFY_AUDIO_SETTINGS` | To play in-game alert sounds with appropriate audio mode |

## Data Storage

- **Location:** `/data/data/com.smiaug.torchzhyla/files/` on your device
- **Encryption:** Not encrypted (locally stored plaintext)
- **Deletion:** Clearing app data or uninstalling deletes all stored information
- **No Backup:** Data is not backed up to Google Cloud

## Server Connections

When you connect to a Telnet server:
- Your connection is sent **unencrypted** (standard Telnet protocol)
- Each server has its own terms and privacy policy
- **We are not responsible** for server content, availability, or how servers handle your data
- Each MUD server operator is responsible for their terms

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Changes take effect immediately upon posting. Your continued use of TorchZhyla constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
